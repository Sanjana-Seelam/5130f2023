We all know the “hidden photos” feature on our phone’s gallery, this option is so interesting yet doesn’t have a feature that helps manage all these photos and gets messy. This research proposal aims to address the challenges faced by users in managing hidden photos within Android gallery apps. The study will further try addressing existing limitations, user challenges and potential privacy concerns related to this feature. 
Specifically, it explores the Secure Folder functionality available on Android (ex: Samsung devices) and the organizational capabilities of Google Photos. this research aims to provide a seamless and secure solution for organizing hidden photos, emphasizing user satisfaction and privacy.
